Basics Step 1: Introduction

Welcome to Politics & War!

This tutorial interface will help you to learn the ropes. You can access this tutorial popup at any time by clicking the Tutorial button in the navigation bar (mobile) or by clicking the "Tutorial" link in the sidebar.

This tutorial is broken into modules which you can select using the tabs at the top of the popup. You can progress through these modules independently.

The tutorial is optional and is mainly intended to be an education tool to guide you through the first 30 days or so of gameplay. You are welcome to deviate from the steps and manage your nation however you like.

To progress through each module, read through each step (or watch the video which will contain the same information) and click the "Continue" button below to move on to the next step.

If you get stuck, have questions, or need help, we highly encourage you to join the Politics & War Discord Server. There, you will be able to ask questions and communicate with game staff and other players in real-time. The channel #game-help in particular exists to assist you.
